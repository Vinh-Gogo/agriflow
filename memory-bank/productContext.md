# Product Context: HydroSense Hub

## Why this project exists
Irrigation management often relies on guesswork or rigid timers that don't account for changing weather. HydroSense Hub solves this by merging real-time sensor data with AI intelligence to ensure plants get exactly the water they need, when they need it.

## Target Audience
- Home gardeners with diverse plant types.
- Small-scale farmers looking for automated efficiency.
- Tech-savvy environmentalists focused on water conservation.

## Key Workflows
1. **Overview**: Monitor system health and active watering events from the main Dashboard.
2. **Zone Management**: Drill down into specific zones to see historical trends and AI-driven "Thirst Index" reasoning.
3. **Scheduling**: Set up complex routines that adapt to environmental conditions (e.g., skip watering if wind speed is too high).
4. **Analysis**: Review weekly AI reports to refine irrigation strategies and detect anomalies like leaks.
